Poor mental health information

What is to have a bad mental health? Unhealthy mental health refers to a state where a person has difficulty coping with daily life, characterized by a range of symptoms that affect their thoughts, emotions, and behaviors.

How know if you have a poor mental health? You may have poor mental health if you experience major changes in your thoughts, feelings, or behaviors, such as persistent sadness, excessive worry, extreme mood swings, or a loss of interest in activities you once enjoyed.
